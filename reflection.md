# Reflection

## High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi
The High-Energy Pop profile recommended more upbeat songs with stronger energy, while the Chill Lofi profile recommended calmer and softer tracks. This makes sense because the two profiles had very different genre, mood, and energy preferences.

## Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock
The Chill Lofi profile favored relaxed and low-energy songs, while the Deep Intense Rock profile pushed more intense songs to the top. However, after the first strong rock match, some non-rock songs still ranked highly because they had similar energy levels.

## Deep Intense Rock vs Sad but High-Energy
These two profiles both preferred high-energy songs, which is why some of the same tracks appeared in both outputs. The main difference is that the rock profile had at least one strong genre match, while the sad but high-energy profile struggled to find songs that matched both mood and energy.

## Most surprising result
The most surprising result was how often the same high-energy songs appeared across different profiles after I increased the weight of energy. This showed me that even small changes in scoring weights can significantly affect the recommendations.
